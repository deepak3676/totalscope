At TotalScope, we prioritize ethical business practices, safety compliance, and open communication with our clients. Our Client Code of Conduct outlines the essential principles and standards that every client must uphold while availing themselves of our services. By checking each of the following boxes, our clients commit to fostering a professional and trustworthy relationship with our company:

Client Code of Conduct
Insurance Fraud
  • Client shall not illegally waive deductibles
  • Client shall not request the release of depreciation for any work not performed
Licensing
  • Client must carry proper licensing for the appropriate jurisdiction where performing work
  • Client must pull permit where required
Insurance
  • Client must carry a minimum of $1,000,000.00 of General Liability Insurance
  • Client must carry worker’s compensation insurance where applicable
Safety
  • Client’s job sites must provide proper personal protective equipment
  • Client must follow OSHA standards and guidelines
Warranties
  • Client must offer and honor workmanship warranties
  • Warranty repairs must be addressed in a timely fashion
Supplements
  • Client must not fraudulently supplement insurance carriers
  • Client will provide photo documentation of every supplement item
Submittals
  • All submittal docs must be provided for every report requested
    • Eagle view or similar will be provided when available
  • Eagle view or similar will be provided when available
  • A scope sheet will be provided for EVERY structure work that is to be performed on
Distributors
  • Client agrees to pay supplier bills on time
Subcontracted Labor
  • Client agrees to pay all subcontracted services on time
